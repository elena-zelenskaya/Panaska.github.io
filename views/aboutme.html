About Me

I am a full-stack developer with a solid technical skill-set. I am passionate about creating new beautiful things with code and seeing the results of my work that are useful to other people.

I studied Maths at the university in Russia, and since that time, I was fascinated with the beauty and misleading simplicity of logic, so I got interested in coding. It also attracted me because I always liked to solve problems and find solutions. I always enjoyed learning, and in web development, you discover something new every day. Many of my skills are self-taught.

Plus, I finished a few web developing classes, and then I started to work as a web developer in an e-learning company that specialized in creating online courses for schools from scratch. We developed online lessons for all grades: from pre-school to complex high school subjects such as chemistry and physics. I liked working there because what can be more important than education? It has (or should have) a crucial part in the lives of all people. Online classes make education closer and more accessible to many people. And I felt my work mattered. When I quit my job a few years later (due to moving to the USA), there were almost two hundred different courses in our online system.

In 2017, I moved to the USA and started working as an associate for Amazon. It was a valuable experience, but I always wanted to get back to web development, so I applied to a Coding Dojo boot camp. It was a very intensive and challenging 20-week full-stack development program during which I learned a lot. Now I feel ready for the next step in my career. I believe that my skills and experience can bring value to the table. So, I would love to participate in some socially important project that makes people's lives better or easier. I am particularly interested in back-end development. I like working with data and databases.
